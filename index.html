Game Rules

Welcome to the Tamagotchi game! Here are the rules:

  • Take care of your pet to ensure a long happy life.
  • Keep an eye on its food, happiness. and sleep levels.
  • If any level of your pets food, happiness, or sleep level reaches 0 your pet will die.
  • Have fun and enjoy taking care of your virtual pet!
